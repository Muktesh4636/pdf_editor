🏦 Filter bank transactions

Upload your bank statement PDF, add one or more people (name can be partial), optional phone numbers, and we show only lines that match. Everything runs in your browser.

How matching works — for each person, a line is kept if the name text appears anywhere in that line (not case-sensitive), or the phone number appears (digits only, partial OK). You can add several people: a line is shown if it matches any of them. Scanned (image-only) PDFs have no text to search — use a text-based statement or the bank’s “download PDF” (not a photo).
